Branding, remembered through sensibility.

Nūfik is a space where I share my design work and the philosophy behind it. My goal is to build brands that don't end at the visual, but feel almost tactile, brands that linger in memory through their mood.

My work centers on visual identity, extending into graphic design, editorial, packaging, UI, key visuals, and the small considered details that hold a brand together. I work closely with passionate founders and teams who carry a clear vision, shaping a visual language that belongs only to their brand.

How to build a visual story

I believe every brand exists in two layers, the essence that stays unseen, and the way it shows itself to the world. I think of these as inner branding and external branding.

Inner branding is about a brand's attitude, philosophy, emotion, and direction. External branding is the process of translating all of that into a visual experience.

Because every brand carries a different message, I believe the most important part of my work is finding the visual language that belongs to each one. I shape a consistent story by considering the brand's philosophy, its mission, and the flow of emotion and experience felt by the people who encounter it.

What I value :

A distinct aesthetic and sensibility. Efficiency. A romantic way of looking at life. Passion, kindness, and humor.

In the age of AI, I find myself drawn to the comfort of imperfection, to honesty, and to experiences that feel more human, more genuine, more sensorial.

About Nūfik

Just as pixels gather to form an image, and halftone dots gather to form a printed page, I believe small ideas, when set in motion, can build entire new worlds.

Nūfik means light (Nū) and creation (fik).

At Nūfik, we craft sensations, turning emotions into visual identities that resonate and stay in memory.
